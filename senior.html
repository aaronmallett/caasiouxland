Senior Community Service Employment Program (SCSEP)

Senior Employment Program

SCSEP provides assistance to people age 55 and older who need a job. We provide participants for area nonprofit organizations that agree to provide on-the-job trianing and supervision. The ultimate goaal is finding the participants unsubsidized jobs. In addition to Woodbury County this program also serves Buena Vista, Cherokee, Ida, Lyon, Plymouth, Sioux and Woodbury.

Senior Service America's Senior Community Service Employment Program

Senior Service America's Senior Community Service Employment Program is funded by a grant from the U.S. Department of Labor Employment & Training Administration. SCSEP is an equal opportunity program.

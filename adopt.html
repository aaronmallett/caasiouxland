Adopt-a-Classroom

According to the National Head Start Association, Head Start has helped more than 32 million children and their families prepare for school and for life. Head Start offers a unique whole child/whole family program design coupled with a delivery system that includes local programs, national standards, monitoring, professional development, and family engagement.

Community individuals, groups and/or organizations looking to provide support to the children we serve within our agency are encouraged to participate by adopting a classroom.  Within Sioux City, we operate 23 classrooms that provide a high quality, developmentally appropriate preschool experience to more than 400 children each year.  Classrooms are continually in need a basic materials for the children - art supplies, books, games, puzzles and other developmentally appropriate items.



If interested, please contact the Community Action Agency of Siouxland's Early Childhood Assistant Director at cbeatty@caasiouxland.org. We will then provide you with additional information on classroom locations and specific materials.
